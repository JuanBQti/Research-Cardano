# NuFi

## Identity

NuFi AG, registered in Baar, Switzerland. Non-custodial wallet and DeFi platform. Browser extension + web wallet supporting 100+ blockchains (EVM + SVM). Users retain ADA ownership and delegate through the wallet UX. Features: built-in staking (Cardano, Solana, Flow), hardware wallet integration (Ledger, Trezor, Keystone), social login, cross-chain swaps, NFT gallery. Claims $220M+ assets staked, 150K+ transactions. Trustpilot 4.7/5.

On Cardano, Koios groups the cluster under the ADALITE surface label (shared with Kiln), but NUFI tickers and nu.fi metadata are first-party.

## Profile

NuFi is a hybrid: it operates pools (like an MPO) but routes delegation through its own wallet product (like a platform). Users retain custody — NuFi does not control the ADA — but the wallet UX mediates pool discovery. 41K delegators at 7.6K ADA each is solidly retail.

At 3.0% margin, NuFi prices at the cell median. The higher operator take (8.9% vs cell average 6.8%) reflects a pool-size distribution that amplifies fixed-cost drag — 17 pools spreading 314M across a fleet means several pools sit below optimal scale.

## Historical trajectory

Peaked strongly at epoch 410 (1.97% of supply) — a wallet adoption wave — then retreated to 0.81% at epoch 618. The decline reflects competitive pressure from other wallet integrations and some delegation migration to Kiln.
