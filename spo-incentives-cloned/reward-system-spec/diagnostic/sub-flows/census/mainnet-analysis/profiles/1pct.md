## Profile

1PCT is the archetype of the community-aligned operator: low margin (1.0% — bottom of the competitive band), no institutional clients, delegation sourced entirely from retail. At 28K ADA/delegator, the user base is mid-retail — larger individual holders than Everstake's mass-market (1.7K) but clearly sovereign delegators.

The operator take of 8.7% — above the cell average (6.8%) despite the lowest margin in the top 10 — reveals the fleet penalty. 18 pools for 268M ADA means an average of ~15M per pool. Several sit below optimal scale, where the 340 ADA fixed cost becomes the dominant extraction channel. The operator's margin philosophy (1%) is undermined by its fleet structure.
