## Profile

YUTA is the fourth-largest entity in the cell but the most opaque. At 152K ADA per delegator, it sits in a grey zone: not custodial at the Coinbase/Blockdaemon scale (millions per address), but far above the retail norm (Everstake 1.7K, NuFi 7.6K). This is consistent with a Japanese exchange or custodial service managing mid-size customer accounts.

At 5.0% margin — tied with Coinbase at the band ceiling — and 10.2% operator take (highest in the top 10), YUTA extracts more than any comparable entity. The high operator take also reflects fleet structure: 23 pools for 431M ADA means several pools are sub-scale, amplifying fixed-cost drag.
