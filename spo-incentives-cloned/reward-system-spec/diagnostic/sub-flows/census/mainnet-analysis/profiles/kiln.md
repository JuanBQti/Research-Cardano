# Kiln

> Website: [kiln.fi](https://www.kiln.fi)

## Identity

Kiln describes itself as "the institutional layer for onchain assets." Enterprise-focused infrastructure provider for staking and yield generation. Supports 30+ PoS networks plus DeFi integrations (Aave, Morpho, Compound). Products include dedicated validators, staking pools, Connect SDK, white-label widgets, OmniVault, and Railnet (yield layer for asset managers).

On Cardano, the Koios surface label is ADALITE (shared with NuFi — reflecting the Adalite wallet as a common front-end). First-party branding via kiln.fi metadata and KILN tickers.

## Profile

Kiln is the second-largest entity by stake in the cell. 445 delegators for 635M ADA (1.43M/deleg) places it squarely in the custodial regime — these are institutional client wallets routed through Kiln's staking infrastructure, not individual delegators browsing a pool list.

The entity serves enterprises, financial institutions, and major wallets. Its Cardano presence is one product line among many — the same infrastructure that runs Ethereum dedicated validators and Solana staking also manages Cardano pools. At 3.0% margin, Kiln sits below Coinbase (5.0%) and Everstake (4.0%) but above the community median.

## Historical trajectory

Steady institutional growth: 0.66% of supply at epoch 400, 1.82% at epoch 618. The most consistent growth trajectory in the IVaaS cohort.
